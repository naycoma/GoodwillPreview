[h1]Features & How to Use[/h1]
Displays faction goodwill changes in the transport pod launch dialog.
Click to open a dropdown menu to select the faction.

輸送ポッドの荷積みダイアログに、各派閥への友好値変動を表示します。
※クリックで表示する派閥を切り替え

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
Save compatible. You are free to add or remove this mod at any time.

No background processing — calculations only run while the window is open.
